Display Description:

This Ushabti of the Late Period has a Plain Lappet wig with 2 bands, plain plinth back-ten horizontal registers of hieroglyphs down the front and glazed in blue faience. The hands are crossed with sleeves with the right hand holding a holding a pick and the left holding a hoe. The hieroglyphs read for "Nesptah priest Sameref, prophet of Isis".
