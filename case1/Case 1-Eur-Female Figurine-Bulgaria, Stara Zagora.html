Discussion: This figurine dates to the beginning of farming in central Europe and their red ochre geometric markings probably emulated the shape of tattoos or body paint. The production of ceramic figurines indicates that Stara Zagora was a sedentary society in which there was expendable time for producing amulets such as this for individuals.
