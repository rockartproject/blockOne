Discussion: Těšetice-Kyjovice is located on the border of the municipalities Kyjovice and Tesetice on the southern slope above the river Únanovkou. It is a multicultural location with the beginning of a Neolithic settlement. It was discovered in 1956, explored since 1958 . Since 1967 there regularly performed systematic research sites.
