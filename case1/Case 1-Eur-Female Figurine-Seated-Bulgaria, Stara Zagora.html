Geographical Area: Stara Zagora, Bulgaria

Cultural Affiliation: Neolithic

Dimensions:

Weight:

Provenance: Archaeological Museum in Stara Zagora

Condition: Archaeological Museum in Stara Zagora Replica

Discussion: This figurine dates to the beginning of farming in central Europe and the red ochre geometric markings probably emulated the shape of tattoos or body paint. The production of Stara Zagora ceramic figurines indicates that this was a sedentary society in which there was expendable time for producing amulets such as this for individuals.
