Case 3-ME-Iraq-Gilgamesh-Palace of Sargon II at Khorsabad

This colossal figure choking a lion once stood at the palace of Assyrian king Sargon II at Khorsabad in northern Iraq. Often identified with the hero Gilgamesh, the statue now resides at the Louvre Museum in Paris. Musée du Louvre, Paris. Gilgamesh subduing a lion, Louvre museum
