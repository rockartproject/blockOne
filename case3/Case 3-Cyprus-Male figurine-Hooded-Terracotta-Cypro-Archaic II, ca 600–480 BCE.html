Display Description:

This Cypriot terracotta represents a male holding a chalice by its stem, and his upwardly facing posture suggests that he is presenting it in a sacred ritual. He is wearing a cloak that covers his body and his head. The figurine is both wheel-made for the central columnar volume of the body and hand-made for distinguishing facial and upper body features. The figurine is solid terracotta, and it has a columnar plinth as its base, suggesting that it was once inserted into a foundation of a terra cotta model.
