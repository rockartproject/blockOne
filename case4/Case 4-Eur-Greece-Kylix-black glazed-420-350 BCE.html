Case 4-Eur-Greece-Kylix-black glazed-420-350 BCE

base ring stem and two handles 20.4 cm wide 7 cm high

An large elegant ancient Greek late Attic/Apulian blackware kylix, dating to approximately 420-350 BCE

Two loop handles lie one on each side.  The piece is decorated with a glossy black glaze.  The central tondo is adorned with palmette decoration
